About me

Born on April 3rd, 1991 and raised in the Ottawa area. Since I was young I always showed interest in creative outlets like drawing and painting. As I got into highschool I became very intrigued with computers and lead me down the path to studying computer systems and learning how they operate. I've been a graffiti and mural artist for the past 8 years. I've studied Marketing, CST and graphic design.

I've participated in group art shows at various galleries in Ottawa and you can find some of my graffiti and murals on the walls of the city and surrounding areas.
